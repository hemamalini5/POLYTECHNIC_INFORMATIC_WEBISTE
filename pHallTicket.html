AP POLYCET HALL TICKET:



The State Board of Technical Education and Training, Vijayawada (Andhra Pradesh) has RELEASED the AP POLYCET hall ticket in online mode. Candidates who have registered successfully can only download the admit card of AP POLYCET from the official website - polycetap.nic.in. Candidates must note that the hall ticket or hall ticket is issued online mode.


The operator at the help line center enters the data given in the APPLICAITION form and uploads the photo and signatures after scanning and gives a print out to the student for verification. If the student is satisfied with the data entered, operator presses the Confirm button, and generates the hall ticket and issues the same to the student.





It is mandatory to carry the hall ticket of AP POLYCET in the entrance exam. Candidates may check the steps mentioned on this page to download the hall ticket. All the candidates need to enter their username and password to download a copy of the hall ticket/ hall ticket. Candidates may refer to the how to apply for AP POLYCET entrance exam in order to know the detailed process of how to apply for this entrance exam conducted by the State Board of Technical Education & Training (SBTET), Andhra Pradesh.


STEPS TO DOWNLOAD AP POLYCET ADMIT CARD/Hall Ticket:


  • Step 1: Visit the official website of AP POLYCET or click here to go website
  • Step 2: Click on the tab highlighting Download the AP POLYCET (2022) admit card.
  • Step 3: Enter your hall ticket number, date of birth, and year of passing/appearing in class 10 including the password to log in.
  • Step 4: Click on the submit tab.
  • Step 5: Download the admit card of AP POLYCET and carry on the exam day.
