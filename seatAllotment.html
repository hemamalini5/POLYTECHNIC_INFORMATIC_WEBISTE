AP POLYCET Seat Allotment:

The State Board of Technical Education and Training, Andhra Pradesh will conduct AP Polycet seat allotment online at appolycet.nic.in. Candidates who pass the AP Polycet will be invited to the counselling and seat allocation process. Document verification, registration, course and college selection, fee payment, and other procedures are part of the AP Polycet counselling and seat allotment process.


The final AP Polycet seat allocation will be based on the candidates' merit score, category, availability of seats, and preferences. The authorities will announce the AP Polycet counselling and seat allotment results online, and candidates must download their allotment letters after paying the fee. Candidates have a higher chance of seat allotment when they fill the AP POLYCET web options correctly and select more options.


AP POLYCET Seat Allotment Process:


Once the AP POLYCET results are published, the counselling process will commence. then seat allotment will be conducted. Candidates can follow the steps below to learn more about AP Polycet counselling and seat allocation.


  1. AP POLYCET registration:

    2. Candidates must register for AP Polycet web-based counselling and seat allotment, as well as pay the processing fee. Once a candidate has registered, he or she can lock in his or her preferences for a specific date.


  2. Verification of documents:

    3. For document verification, all eligible candidates must report to AP Polycet helpline centres. At the centres, candidates must present all original and photocopied documents. Candidates will be given a 'Receipt of Certificates' once all of their documents have been confirmed.


  3. Web Option Entry:

    4. Candidates must enter their polytechnic and course choices online on the scheduled day of web option entry. Candidates must print their option entry form after locking their choices of colleges from the list of AP POLYCET participating colleges. For the option entry process, candidates can also visit AP Polycet helpline centres.


  4. AP Polycet Seat Allotment

    5. After candidates have entered their options, the authorities will consider their choices, category, and merit rank before releasing the final allotment. Following that, officials will announce the AP Polycet seat allotment results. By logging into the portal, candidates will be able to check the status of their seat allotment. Candidates who have been assigned seats must download their AP Polycet seat allotment letters from the portal after paying a Rs 800 fee.


  5. Reporting to the designated centres and payment of fees

    6. Once candidates have downloaded their AP Polycet seat allotment letters, they must report to their assigned college, first electronically and then physically. It should be noted that both online and paper reporting are required. Following that, candidates must pay the required admission fee.

  • Candidates will then be able to see the AP POLYCET seat allotment result
