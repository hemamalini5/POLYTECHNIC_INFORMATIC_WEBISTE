Web Counselling:


For the Convenience of the Candidates from the academic year 2009-10, Government has decided to adopt a total web based counseling procedure where a candidate can exercise options i.e. candidate can select a polytechnic and course which he/she intends to study, through internet centre or internet facility available at his/her residence and where access to such facility is not available, from any one of the help line centers established by Govt in the districts. Such help line centers will be available in each district.


Steps to be followed in AP POLYCET Web Counselling:


  • Step 1: Pay Processing Fee Online for AP POLYCET Admission Counselling
  • Step 2: Slot Booking for AP POLYCET Certificate Verification
  • Step 3: Attend Certificate Verification at HLC where slot is booked
  • Step 4: Candidates Registration
  • Step 5: Candidates Login for Web Option Entry
  • Step 6: Print List of Saved Options

Web Counselling Stages

AP POLYCET Web Counseling Process involves, Slot booking, Certificate Verification, Web Option Entry. POLYCET official will give detailed instructions for Web counseling process for admission into polytechnics. Soon after the declaration of results of SSC and POLYCET, detailed notification will be published in daily newspapers inviting the students to attend for certificate verification and option entry. The details of web based counseling and venues for certificate verification and option entry will be made available in web site https://appolycet.nic.in/.


Stage 1: Issue of Web Counselling Notification:


AP POLYCET Web Counselling Notification: The Qualified candidates of A.P POLYCET who are desirous of seeking admission into Diploma Courses are informed that the web based counseling process comprising of Certificate Verification and Exercising of Web Options.


The Director of Technical Education (DTE) issues a Web Counselling notification every year, during first week of June, in all prominent news papers indicating the date wise ranks of the candidates who have to attend for Registration, Verification of Certificates and Schedule for Exercising options and DTE will also notify the list of Help-line Centres. Candidates who fail to report for registration and Certificates verification will not be considered for allotment of seats in any of the polytechnics.


The Candidate has to pay the Processing fee through online using :


appolycet.nic.in
  • Click on the Pay fee online button.
  • Enter POLYCET-2022 Hall Ticket number, Rank and Captcha

  • The candidate is directed to payment gateway for payment of processing fee.


  • Select Credit card / Debit card / Net banking option through which candidate wants to pay the fee and enter your bank account or card credentials and execute the payment by entering the OTP received to your mobile.
  • After a successful transaction, an SMS is sent to the mobile.
  • Note down the reference number for future reference or take a print out of the fee payment receipt by click on Print

Stage 2: Certificate Verification


Candidates called on each day for counseling shall attend with all original certificates and documents for verification at any one of the designated venues. Candidate shall check the details such as local area, reservation/special reservation category; sex printed on the receipt of certificates and gets them corrected by the verification officer immediately.




Candidates should produce the following certificates. Candidates will be issued a receipt of Certificates at Help-line Centres after Verification. Officials would be given User Id and Password details after verification for registration to give web options


Stage 3: Exercising Web Options


After Certificate Verification in Help-line Centres, a Blank Option Form (speciman), List of Polytechnics with the courses offered by them and course codes will be given to all the Candidates. Based on the Course Codes and polytechnic Codes, Candidates may list out polytechnic in order of preference, the courses which they intend to give options for. For the reference of the candidates , the last rank up to which candidates were admitted in different polytechnics/courses in the previous academic year would be made availble to candidates on the web site.


The candidate should first prepare a priority number with a list of courses in the blank option form before he/she attempts to enter the options on the web. Candidates can exercise any number of options. Candidates will be familiarized with the web counseling procedure and the relevant information with regard to the seats available and college codes will be given. Candidates have to prepare a set of all possible college-course combinations (called OPTIONS) they wish to join in consultation with their parents, teachers, and friends in the manual option form supplied.


The order of options so prepared is to be entered in the web by login through a generated password. Candidates shall enter as many number of options as possible they wish to join to avoid disappointment of not getting a seat. A print out of the options so entered shall be kept as record for reference. Options recorded on the last day of the counseling will be frozen and will be taken for processing of allotments.


After the finalising the options, he is advised to take print out of the final option form for his/her record. Help-Line Centres shall demonstrate the detailed procedure of Exercising Options immediately after the certificate verification.


Stage 4: Final Allotments of Seats:


Based on the options exercised, seat allotment will be made as per merit rank and category (SC/ST/BC/PH/NCC/CAP/Sports and games quota etc ) of the candidate. The final Allotments will be placed on the Web on the scheduled date announced before hand and the Candidates will have to down load the allotment order from the web site.


Allotment of seats will be based on a computer program considering the merit of the candidate in all possible reservation categories of a candidate and the best possible seats among the eligible options will be allotted. Candidates shall download the allotment order and challan for payment of fee through any branch of the designated banks.


Stage 5: Payment of Fees & Reporting at Polytechnic


Candidates, with downloaded allotment order, have to remit the fee in one of the designated banks and obtain a receipt. After that candidate with allotment order and Fee receipts have to report at the respective Polytechnics where the Seat is allotted on or before the dates specified in the allotment order.


Candidates shall report at the allotted college along with the allotment order and fee paid challan within the prescribed time. Candidates who fail to turn up for the counseling on the Notified date and time, not reporting in time at the allotted college or not paying the fee through challan will have no claim on the admission into polytechnics.


If Candidate fails to report for admission at the allotted polytechnic by the dates stipulated, the allotment stands cancelled and candidate will have no claim for further allotment of seats. Calling for counseling does not guarantee a seat, as more number of candidates than the seats available, will be called for. The Convenor is fully empowered to disqualify a candidate seeking admission if Admission Officer is not satisfied with the documents produced by the Candidate. The decision of Convenor is final in matters of granting seat / rejecting seat to a candidate called for counseling.


The candidate shall attend the counseling at their own cost and if necessary, they may have to stay a day more at the station of Admission Camp as advised by the Admission Officer. The selection of ST/SC/BC/CAP/PH/NCC/SPORTS candidates is subject to the clearance to be given by the respective departments during the counseling. Convener is not responsible for the delay in clearance in respect of doubtful caste cases.
