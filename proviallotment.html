STEPS FOR DOWNLOADING PROVISIONAL ALLOTMENT ORDER:


steps that the candidates must follow in order to download the AP Polycet provisional allotment letter:


  • The Provisional Seat Allotment letter for AP Polycet will be updated on the official AP Polycet website.
  • The candidates have to enter the valid details on the student's login page such as Hall Ticket number, ICR Form Number, Date of Birth and Password and press the Submit button.
  • The Provisional Allotment Letter will be displayed on the computer screen.
  • The candidates have to download the same and carry a coloured printout of the same for the purpose of admission.
