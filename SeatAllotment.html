AP POLYCET Spot Round Seat Allotment Process:


The spot round seat allotment process will be conducted by the authorities in case there are still a certain number of seats remains to be filled after the Web-Based counselling rounds concludes. The candidates must fulfil the following eligibility criteria in order to be considered for the AP Polycet spot round process:


  • Should have passed AP Polycet entrance exam.
  • Should not have joined any Polytechnic Institute via the Convenor AP Polycet Admissions.
  • Did not participate in the process of documents verification
  • Did not qualify the AP Polycet exam.
  • Did not appear in the AP Polycet entrance exam.
