AP POLYCET RESULTS:


AP POLYCET Result:




The exam authorities will declare the result of AP POLYCET at the official website. Candidates will have to use their AP POLYCET hall ticket number to download the results. The AP POLYCET result includes details like the overall and subject-wise score obtained by the candidate in the entrance exam


How to check AP POLYCET Rank Card :


  • Log-in to result portal using AP POLYCET hall ticket number
  • The rank card will be displayed on the screen
  • Download and take a printout of the rank card.
  • Details mentioned in AP Polycet Rank Card

AP POLYCET Result - Details Mentioned:

  • Candidate's name
  • Hall ticket number
  • Subject-wise marks
  • Aggregate marks
  • Qualifying status of the candidate



Minimum Qualifying Marks of AP Polycet:


To qualify in the exam, candidates from open category need to score a minimum of 30% marks or obtain 36 marks out of 120. No minimum qualifying marks were set for SC and ST candidates and they will be included in the merit list irrespective of their scores.


AP POLYCET Result - Tie-breaking rule:


In cases where two or more candidates score the same marks, the tie will be decided in the following order:


  • Candidates who have higher marks in mathematics will be ranked higher
  • Candidates with higher marks in physics will be placed higher
  • If the tie persists, the candidate older in age will be given preference.
