AP POLYCET Web Options:



The web options for AP POLYCET released by the authorities and the candidates who have successfully registered through the official website are eligible for exercising the web options. Only those candidates whose data is verified online or certificates verified through helpline centres will be allowed to login and choose web options. The AP POLYCET web options will be available for the candidates who appeared for AP POLYCET and got their rank cards. The candidates must have a clear idea of colleges & codes to exercise web options. All the details with regard to the AP POLYCET web options can be checked on this pag.

Steps to Exercise Web Options for AP POLYCET




Here are the detailed steps to exercise the web options for AP POLYCET -


  • Step 1: Visit the official website appolycet.nic.in
  • Step 2: Click on 'Candidate Registration' to generate a password. For generating the password, the candidates will have to enter the ICR Form Number, AP POLYCET Hall Ticket Number, Rank, Date of Birth, and Captcha. Click on the 'Generate Password' button.
  • Step 3: Create a password now. The password should be a combination of alphabets and numbers. Example - kumar123, radha456, rajesh2003 etc.
  • Step 4: Save the Password and Logout
  • Step 5: Login to exercise the web options. You need to click on option entry. An OTP will be received on the registered mobile number.
  • Step 6: Enter the OTP. Now, select the desired district. The candidates should select any number of districts. The list of colleges in the respective districts with codes will be displayed.

  • Step 7: On the option entry form, you will have to choose the colleges and courses. A priority number such as 1,2,3,4... should be marked for each college and course.
  • Step 8: Now, Save/ Confirm the options and Logout.
